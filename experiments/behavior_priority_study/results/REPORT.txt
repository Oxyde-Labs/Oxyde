BEHAVIOR PRIORITY STUDY - RESULTS
================================================================================

Overview
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This experiment compares emotion-modulated behavior selection against
traditional fixed-priority and random selection approaches.

Key Findings
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

RANDOM SELECTION
  Variety Score: 2.278
  Match Rate: 70.6%
  Avg Priority Override: 5.88

EMOTION MODULATED
  Variety Score: 1.808
  Match Rate: 47.1%
  Avg Priority Override: 8.24

FIXED PRIORITY
  Variety Score: 1.954
  Match Rate: 76.5%
  Avg Priority Override: 5.88

Conclusions
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Variety Difference: -7.5%
2. Match Rate Difference: -29.4%
3. Emotional Influence: 8.24 avg modifier
